Thanos - The Destroyer



Who is Thanos?

Thanos is one of the most powerful and feared villains in the Marvel Universe. A ruthless warlord from Titan, he seeks ultimate power to reshape the universe according to his will.

1. Comic Book Origins:

  • First appeared in Iron Man #55 (1973), created by **Jim Starlin**.
  • A member of the **Eternals**, born on Titan with the **Deviant Syndrome**, making him larger and more powerful.
  • Became obsessed with **Death**, literally falling in love with the entity.
  • Seeks power through the **Infinity Stones** to wipe out half of all life in the universe.

2. Powers & Abilities:

  • Superhuman strength, durability, and intelligence.
  • Master strategist and combatant.
  • Wields the **Infinity Gauntlet**, making him virtually omnipotent.
  • Energy projection, telepathy, and matter manipulation.

3. Thanos in the MCU:

  • Portrayed by **Josh Brolin** in the Marvel Cinematic Universe.
  • First appeared in *The Avengers (2012)* (Post-credits scene).
  • Guardians of the Galaxy (2014) – Plans to collect the Infinity Stones.
  • Avengers: Infinity War (2018) – Successfully wipes out half the universe.
  • Avengers: Endgame (2019) – The Avengers reverse his snap and defeat him.
