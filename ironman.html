IRON MAN




Who is Iron Man?

Iron Man is the superhero persona of **Anthony Edward "Tony" Stark**, a billionaire genius, philanthropist, and weapons manufacturer who transforms into one of Earth's greatest heroes after an unexpected crisis.

1. Comic Book Origins:

  • First appeared in Tales of Suspense #39 (1963), created by **Stan Lee, Larry Lieber, Don Heck, and Jack Kirby**.
  • After being **captured by terrorists**, Tony builds a powered suit of armor to escape.
  • Decides to use his technology for **good instead of war**.
  • Becomes a founding member of **The Avengers**.

2. Powers & Abilities:

  • Genius-level intellect with advanced engineering skills.
  • Powered suits grant **superhuman strength, flight, and energy projection**.
  • Expert strategist and hand-to-hand combatant.
  • Has built numerous **Iron Man armors**, including the **Hulkbuster and nanotech suits**.

3. Iron Man in the MCU:

  • Portrayed by **Robert Downey Jr.** in the Marvel Cinematic Universe.
  • Iron Man (2008) – Origin story and creation of the first suit.
  • Avengers (2012) – Joins Earth's Mightiest Heroes.
  • Iron Man 3 (2013) – Faces the Mandarin and overcomes PTSD.
  • Captain America: Civil War (2016) – Conflict over superhero accountability.
  • Avengers: Endgame (2019) – Sacrifices himself to defeat Thanos.
