Captain Marvel

Who is Captain Marvel?

Captain Marvel, also known as Carol Danvers, is a powerful superhero in the Marvel Universe.

1. Comic Book Origins:

  • First appeared in Marvel Super-Heroes #13 (1968).
  • Originally a U.S. Air Force pilot, Carol Danvers gained superpowers after an explosion involving Kree technology.
  • Her DNA merged with Kree warrior Mar-Vell’s, giving her incredible abilities.
  • Adopted the title "Captain Marvel" in 2012.

2. Powers & Abilities:

  • Superhuman strength, speed, and durability.
  • Energy absorption and manipulation.
  • Flight and cosmic awareness.
  • Can shoot photon blasts from her hands.

3. Captain Marvel in the MCU:

  • Portrayed by Brie Larson in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU).
  • Captain Marvel (2019) – Origin story and battle against the Skrulls.
  • Avengers: Endgame (2019) – Joins the fight against Thanos.
  • The Marvels (2023) – Teams up with Kamala Khan (Ms. Marvel) and Monica Rambeau.
