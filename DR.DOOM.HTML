Doctor Doom



Who is Doctor Doom?

Doctor Doom, also known as **Victor Von Doom**, is one of the most intelligent and dangerous villains in the Marvel Universe. A ruler, scientist, and sorcerer, he seeks absolute power and dominion over all.

1. Comic Book Origins:

  • First appeared in Fantastic Four #5 (1962), created by **Stan Lee and Jack Kirby**.
  • Born in **Latveria**, he was a brilliant scientist with a tragic past.
  • Blames **Reed Richards (Mr. Fantastic)** for an experiment gone wrong that scarred his face.
  • Becomes the ruler of Latveria, creating advanced technology and wielding powerful magic.

2. Powers & Abilities:

  • Genius-level intellect – one of Marvel’s smartest characters.
  • Master of both science and sorcery.
  • Wears a nearly indestructible suit of armor.
  • Energy projection, force fields, and dimensional travel.
  • Strategic mastermind with an iron grip over Latveria.

3. Doctor Doom in Movies & Future in the MCU:

  • Previously appeared in **Fantastic Four (2005, 2007) & 2015 Reboot**.
  • Rumored to be a major villain in the upcoming **MCU’s Fantastic Four**.
  • Potential to be a future **Avengers-level threat**, rivaling Kang and Thanos.
