Scarlet Witch



Who is Scarlet Witch?

Scarlet Witch, also known as Wanda Maximoff, is a powerful sorceress and mutant in Marvel Comics and the MCU.

1. Comic Book Origins:

  • First appeared in X-Men #4 (1964), created by Stan Lee and Jack Kirby.
  • Originally introduced as a villain alongside her brother Pietro (Quicksilver), part of Magneto’s Brotherhood of Evil Mutants.
  • Later became a long-time member of the Avengers.
  • Possesses reality-warping and chaos magic abilities.

2. Powers & Abilities:

  • Reality manipulation – can alter the fabric of reality itself.
  • Chaos magic – granted by the demon Chthon.
  • Telekinesis and telepathy.
  • Energy projection and force fields.
  • Hex bolts – used to cause unpredictable events.

3. Scarlet Witch in the MCU:

  • Portrayed by Elizabeth Olsen in the Marvel Cinematic Universe.
  • Avengers: Age of Ultron (2015) – Introduced alongside Quicksilver.
  • Captain America: Civil War (2016) – Joins Team Cap.
  • Avengers: Infinity War (2018) – Fights Thanos.
  • WandaVision (2021) – Unlocks full potential as the Scarlet Witch.
  • Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness (2022) – Becomes a major antagonist.
