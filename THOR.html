THE THOR

Thor: The God of Thunder

1. Mythological Origins:

  • Thor is a Norse god from Scandinavian mythology.
  • God of thunder, lightning, storms, strength, and protection.
  • Son of Odin (the All-Father) and Jörð (the earth goddess).
  • Wields the enchanted hammer Mjolnir, granting immense power.

2. Marvel Comics Version:

  • First appeared in Journey into Mystery #83 (1962), created by Stan Lee, Larry Lieber, and Jack Kirby.
  • Prince of Asgard, son of Odin and Frigga.
  • Banished to Earth by Odin to learn humility, living as Dr. Donald Blake.
  • Member of The Avengers, fighting alongside Iron Man, Captain America, and Hulk.
  • Portrayed by Chris Hemsworth in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU).

3. Powers & Abilities:

  • Superhuman strength, speed, and durability.
  • Controls thunder and lightning.
  • Wields Mjolnir, which only the worthy can lift.
  • Flight and dimensional travel.
  • Extended lifespan due to his Asgardian nature.

4. Notable Stories & Movies:

  • Thor (2011) – Origin story in the MCU.
  • The Avengers (2012) – Joins Earth's Mightiest Heroes.
  • Thor: Ragnarok (2017) – Faces Hela and loses Mjolnir.
  • Avengers: Endgame (2019) – Time heist and battle against Thanos.
  • Thor: Love and Thunder (2022) – Introduces Mighty Thor (Jane Foster).
