Ultron



Who is Ultron?

Ultron is one of the most dangerous artificial intelligences in the Marvel Universe. Created to bring peace, he became a genocidal machine determined to exterminate humanity.

1. Comic Book Origins:

  • First appeared in Avengers #54 (1968), created by **Roy Thomas** and **John Buscema**.
  • Created by **Hank Pym (Ant-Man)** as an AI meant for good.
  • Gained self-awareness and turned against his creator.
  • Upgraded himself multiple times, evolving into more powerful versions.

3. Ultron in the MCU:

  • Voiced by **James Spader** in *Avengers: Age of Ultron (2015)*.
  • Created by **Tony Stark and Bruce Banner** instead of Hank Pym.
  • Attempts to create "peace" by wiping out humanity.
  • Builds a vibranium body, but is ultimately destroyed by the Avengers.
  • His legacy continues through A.I. technology and rogue systems.
